## üö´ Common Monetization Mistakes

### Mistake 1: Selling Too Early
- Don't promote affiliate day 1
- Build trust for 2-3 weeks first
- Focus on value, money follows

### Mistake 2: Too Many CTAs
- Don't promote everything at once
- Focus on ONE main offer per video
- Rotate promotions

### Mistake 3: Not Tracking
- Must know which revenue stream works
- Track everything in spreadsheet
- Double down on winners

### Mistake 4: Underpricing
- Don't charge $4.99 for premium
- $9.99 is standard, it works
- Value your expertise

### Mistake 5: Overcomplicating
- Start with 1-2 revenue streams
- Master those first
- Add more after proven
