- Visual proof (show PropStats)

- Clear and simple: "Link in bio"
- Engagement: "Comment below"
- Follow: "Follow for results tomorrow"
- Don't beg, just direct

## Voice & Tone Guidelines

### Do:
- Be confident (not cocky)
- Be educational (not condescending)
- Show proof (receipts, data)
- Be transparent (show losses too)
- Use energy (not boring)

### Don't:
- Guarantee wins (illegal in some places)
- Use complex jargon
- Ramble without point
- Hide losses
- Beg for follows
